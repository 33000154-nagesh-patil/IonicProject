Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) Systematic Investment Plan is an investment strategy where in an investor needs to invest the same amount of money in a particular mutual fund at every stipulated time period. One Time (Lumpsum) One Time, it means a single, bulk amount locked into a one-time mutual fund investment. This is as opposed to spreading it out over time, like in SIP (Systematic Investment Plans). Payment Instant Order Placement Aqube verifies the investment details. Valid transactions are then sent to mutual fund company for further processing. Same day Unit Allotment Mutual Fund company allots units at the applicable price of a unit of the scheme (ie. NAV). Applicable NAV is based on the type of scheme (liquid / non-liquid) Same day* Portfolio Update Mutual Fund company confirms the investment status, units allotted and applicable NAV to Aqube. Based on the confirmation, Aqube updates your portfolio on the app 2 - 3 business days Note *Purchase transactions via UPI (all banks) and NB (ICICI, HDFC, Axis, Kotak, IDBI, SBI & Yes Bank) will be processed on the same day if completed before the cut-off time (12.00 PM for liquid & overnight schemes and 02.00 PM for all others). Investments via other modes or beyond the cut-off time will be processed when the mutual fund company gets money in the bank account. HeyTorus submits the orders to mutual fund companies only if they are valid in terms of KYC information, bank verification & payment status.
